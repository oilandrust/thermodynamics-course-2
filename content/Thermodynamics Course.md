## Learning objectives

After working through this mini-course you should be able to:

1. Apply the first law to closed systems: $\Delta U = Q - W$
2. Relate pressure, volume, and temperature for ideal gases
3. Explain why entropy tends to increase in isolated systems
4. Compute the maximum efficiency of a [[Heat Engines and Carnot Cycle|Carnot engine]]
